About College

C. V. Raman Global University is an engineering and management institution in the Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India. It was established in 1997 as CV Raman College of Engineering under C.V. Raman Group of Institutions. The institute is accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA). The college certifies to ISO 9001:2000. The institute is rated as “A” by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) and is affiliated to Biju Patnaik University of Technology (BPUT). It ranks amongst the top 5 engineering colleges in Odisha. On 29 February 2020, the college was granted university status and was renamed as C. V. Raman Global University.
